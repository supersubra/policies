Privacy Policy for impfo

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

impfo ("we", "our", "us") is a personal warranty management application that allows users to store warranty bills securely in the cloud. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Images of warranty slips and bills
  • Product details entered manually or extracted using OCR
  • Optional notes, tags, and reminders
  • Account information (email, authentication ID)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type, OS version, app version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Usage statistics (anonymized)

3. Camera & OCR Usage

  • Camera access is used only to capture warranty documents
  • Images are processed using on-device or cloud OCR to extract text
  • Images and extracted text are stored securely in your private cloud space
  • We do not access your camera without your permission

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To store and manage your warranties
  • To provide reminders before warranty expiry
  • To improve app reliability and performance
  • To provide customer support

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Google Cloud)
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access is protected using secure authentication

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties.

Data is shared only with trusted infrastructure providers:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, storage, database, analytics
  • Google ML Kit — OCR text extraction

7. Your Rights

  • You can view, edit, or delete your data anytime
  • You may delete your account and all stored data from within the app
  • You may revoke camera permissions at any time in device settings

8. Children's Privacy

impfo is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in regions where Google Cloud operates servers.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date.
